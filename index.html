Zoonotic Diseases from Pets

Rabies

Rabies is a viral disease affecting the nervous system, usually transmitted through animal bites.

Prevention: Vaccinate pets, avoid stray animals, and seek medical help after bites.

Leptospirosis

Leptospirosis is a bacterial disease spread through contaminated water, affecting kidneys and liver.

Prevention: Keep pets away from contaminated water, maintain hygiene, and vaccinate them.

Toxoplasmosis

Toxoplasmosis is a parasitic infection from cat feces, dangerous for pregnant women.

Prevention: Wash hands after handling cats, clean litter boxes properly, and cook meat well.

Ringworm

Ringworm is a contagious fungal skin infection that spreads through direct contact.

Prevention: Keep pets clean, disinfect surfaces, and treat infections early.

Hantavirus

Hantavirus is a severe respiratory disease caused by exposure to rodent urine or droppings.

Prevention: Avoid contact with rodents, seal homes, and maintain cleanliness.
